About

I started working in visual effects in 2008 and have worked on numerous film and commercial projects since then. Some of the top-listed films I've contributed towards are The Avengers, Captain America, Thor2, and Oz the Great and Powerful. I am most often hired as a compositor, but I also have experience as a matte painter and Nuke tool developer. These other experiences developed my technical and artistic abilities so that I am able to handle some of the more difficult tasks on a show!

My BFA comes from The Rhode Island School of Design where I graduated with honors in 2005. Afterwards I studied visual effects at The Academy of Art University in San Francisco through their master's program.

If you'd like more information, please don't hesitate to contact me at suenelsonvfx@gmail.com.
